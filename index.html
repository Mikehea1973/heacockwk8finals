Animation techniques

Six motion treatments displayed on one page.

01. Staggered intro

The hero heading enters in sequenced lines to establish rhythm immediately.

02. Floating panels

Accent surfaces drift gently to keep the layout active without overwhelming it.

03. Shimmer sweep

Each card carries a light sweep that simulates gloss moving across a print.

04. Pulse rings

Soft radial pulses reinforce the photography theme’s ambient studio light.

05. Hover tilt

Interactive cards lift and rotate slightly when hovered to signal depth.

06. Drawn lines

Animated line work frames content and echoes layout guides from print design.
